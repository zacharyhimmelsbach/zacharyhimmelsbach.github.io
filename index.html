--- layout: default title: Zach Himmelsbach ---

PhD Student, Harvard University

My work extends econometric analyses of education and labor policies with natural language processing techniques and other data science tools. Using these methods, I explore the substantive impact of school accountability policies on administrative planning; the amount of innovation in the charter sector and how it changes over time; and how to automate the measurement of teaching practices at scale with machine learning. I have also studied attendance-boosting interventions in a multi-site RCT and written about standardized admissions testing (gated/ungated).
